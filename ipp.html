IPP (Independent Power Producer)

IPP is a zero-investment model where SG Green Asset develops, owns and operates grid-connected solar projects and supplies clean energy to businesses under long-term PPAs.

With IPP, businesses benefit from renewable energy without upfront investment. SG Green Asset manages financing, installation, and long-term operation, ensuring clients receive reliable and sustainable power while achieving cost savings and ESG goals.
