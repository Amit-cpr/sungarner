Company Overview

SunGarner Green Asset — Powering the Energy Transition

SunGarner Green Asset is the dedicated subsidiary of SunGarner Energies Ltd, established with the vision to accelerate clean energy adoption through innovative ownership and investment models.

It enables businesses, institutions, and communities to adopt renewable energy solutions without heavy upfront investments, offering RESCO, OPEX, and green asset ownership models.

Vision: To be a leading force in India’s renewable energy transformation.

Mission: To design, develop and deliver reliable green energy solutions.

We Deliver Sustainable Energy Solutions

IPP (Independent Power Producer)

IPP is a zero-investment model where SG Green Asset develops, owns and operates grid-connected solar projects and supplies clean energy to businesses under long-term PPAs.

CPP (Captive Power Plant)
